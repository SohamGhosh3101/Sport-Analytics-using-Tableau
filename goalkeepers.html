Profile

Goalkeepers, often referred to as keepers or goalies, are the players in football (soccer) who are positioned in front of the goal and have the primary responsibility of preventing the opposing team from scoring. Their main objective is to stop shots on goal and make saves using their hands, arms, or any part of their body within the penalty area.
