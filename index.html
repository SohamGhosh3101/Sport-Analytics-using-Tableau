Introduction


Football is a sport renowned for its ability to captivate millions of fans worldwide, captivating their hearts and minds with its thrilling displays of skill, teamwork, and determination. At the heart of every successful football team lies a solid foundation of talented players, and the discovery of young prospects with immense potential is an essential aspect of the game. Scouting young talent is an indispensable practice in football, as it allows clubs to identify and nurture promising players who can shape the future of the sport. By meticulously observing and evaluating young athletes, clubs can unearth hidden gems, cultivate their abilities, and ultimately build formidable teams capable of achieving long-term success. The importance of scouting young talented players in football cannot be overstated, as it serves as a catalyst for continuous growth, innovation, and the preservation of football's rich legacy.


Prospect of Sport Analytics

What is Sports Analytics? Sports Analytics is an emerging field in data science & visualization that has begun to be adopted by teams in all kinds of sports played across the world. Data on players and game are usually collected and analyzed to - Identify future talent, understanding patterns in the playing styles of opponents, providing feedback to talent and sports betting.

Here, we have focused on analysing the promising talents across the globe and have looked deep into each positions of the football pitch. The goal for such an analysis is to list out the favourable candidate for a club, based on their:

  • Statistics
  • Potential
  • Market value
  • Wage Demands
  • Longetivity of the contract with their current club

About the dataset

Football/Soccer is one of the world's most popular sports in the world with 3.5 Billion people watching it every year. With the amount of popularity and money involved, teams are always on the lookout for new upcoming talent. Hence for this project, we are analyzing a dataset of around 18,944 soccer players from sofifa.com by web scraping in the pursuit of identifying the best talents under the age of 22 who fit a set of criteria provided by the analyst of any sports club.

This dataset consists of about 106 features and around 18,944 rows of tuple values, each containing in depth details about the footballer.

The tool used to create the visualization is Tableau. Tableau is a data visualization and business intelligence software that allows users to create interactive and visually appealing dashboards, reports, and charts. It provides a user-friendly interface for analyzing and presenting data from various sources.

Who will benefit?

This section analysis outlines the specific individuals or groups that will gain value and advantages from the project's findings and insights. In the context of analyzing young talent scouting in football, potential beneficiaries may include:

  • Football Clubs and Scouts: The visualization project can provide football clubs and scouts with valuable information on young players' performance, potential, and suitability for their teams. It can assist them in identifying promising talents, making informed decisions during player recruitment, and optimizing their scouting efforts.

  • Coaches and Academy Staff: The project's findings can benefit coaches and staff working in football academies by offering insights into the strengths and weaknesses of young players. This information can help them tailor training programs, provide individualized coaching, and support player development.

  • Young Players and their Agents: The visualization project can empower young players and their agents by highlighting their performance metrics and showcasing their abilities to potential suitors.

  • Football Governing Bodies and Associations: The project's analysis can be valuable for football governing bodies and associations responsible for talent development and player identification. It can aid in the formulation of policies, strategies, and programs aimed at nurturing young talent, improving youth scouting systems, and promoting fair opportunities for aspiring players.

  • Fans and Enthusiasts: The visualization project can engage football fans and enthusiasts by providing them with a deeper understanding of young talent scouting processes and the potential stars of the future. It can generate excitement, discussion, and anticipation around up-and-coming players, adding an extra layer of interest to the sport.
